Viewing the HTML source of a web page in Chrome

"Looking Behind the Curtain"

  1. Open Chrome on your computer.
  2. Open the webpage of your choice.
  3. Click on "View" on your computer's menu bar.
  4. Hover on the "Developer" tab, and click on "View Source".
  5. You can also right click, press the "Inspect" tab, and find the "html" tab in your pop-up window.
